Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about teaching with us.

How much can I earn as an instructor?

Instructors typically earn between $1,000 to $10,000+ per month, depending on course popularity, pricing, and marketing efforts. We offer up to 80% revenue share and provide tools to help maximize your earnings.

What equipment do I need to get started?

You can start with basic equipment: a computer, a good microphone, and a webcam. We provide detailed guides on setting up your recording space and offer recommendations for affordable equipment upgrades.

How much time does it take to create a course?

Most instructors spend 40-80 hours creating their first course. We provide templates and support to streamline the process. Once created, courses can generate passive income for years.

Do I need teaching experience?

While teaching experience is helpful, it's not required. We provide training and resources to help you become an effective online instructor. What matters most is your expertise and passion for your subject.
