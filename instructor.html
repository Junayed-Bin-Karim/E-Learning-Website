Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about teaching with Junayed Academy.

What's the difference between standard and premium instructors?

Premium instructors receive higher revenue shares (up to 85%), dedicated success managers, priority support, advanced analytics, and featured placement on our platform. They also get access to our production studio and marketing support.

How long does it take to get approved as an instructor?

Standard applications are reviewed within 3-5 business days. For premium applications with complete documentation, we expedite the process to 24-48 hours. We'll keep you informed at every step of the review process.

What kind of support do you provide for course creation?

We provide comprehensive support including course planning templates, video production guidance, content review, quality assurance, and platform training. Premium instructors also get access to our production studio and professional editing services.

Can I teach multiple courses?

Absolutely! Many of our top instructors teach 3-5 courses across related topics. We encourage diversifying your course portfolio to maximize earnings and reach different student segments.
