Executive Summary

Empowering Bangladesh Through Accessible Education

Founded in 2024 by Junayed Bin Karim, Junayed Academy is a premier e-learning platform dedicated to transforming technology education. We bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application.

Our mission is to make high-quality, industry-relevant tech learning accessible to students from all backgrounds. To empower every Bangladeshi learner with industry-relevant technical skills through innovative, accessible, and practical education that transforms careers and contributes to national digital growth.

  • Bridge the industry-academia gap
  • Foster practical, project-based learning

Our Vision

To become Bangladesh's leading catalyst for digital transformation by creating a generation of tech professionals who drive innovation, entrepreneurship, and economic growth.

  • Expand to 5 major cities across Bangladesh
