Who We Are

Empowering the Nation through Digital Skills

Founded in 2024 by Junayed Bin Karim, Junayed Academy started with a simple mission: to make premium tech education accessible to everyone in Bangladesh, regardless of their background.

We realized that traditional education often lags behind industry demands. Our platform fills that void by offering project-based learning, mentorship from industry experts, and a community that fosters growth.

Our Mission

To democratize quality tech education in Bangladesh by providing affordable, industry-standard training that leads to real career outcomes.

Practical, project-based curriculum
Direct mentorship from experts

Our Vision

To be the catalyst for Bangladesh's digital transformation, creating a workforce of 10,000+ skilled professionals by 2030.

Nationwide accessibility
Global career opportunities
