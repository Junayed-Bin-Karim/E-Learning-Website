Our Story

Empowering Bangladesh Through Accessible Tech Education

Executive Summary

Founded in 2024 by Junayed Bin Karim, Junayed Academy is a premier e-learning platform dedicated to transforming technology education in Bangladesh. Our mission is to make high-quality, industry-relevant tech learning accessible to students from all backgrounds.

By combining interactive online courses, project-based learning, and expert mentorship, we bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world industry skills. Junayed Academy empowers learners to acquire the competencies needed to thrive in todayâ€™s rapidly evolving tech landscape.

Our Mission

To empower every Bangladeshi learner with industry-relevant technical skills through innovative, accessible, and practical education that transforms careers and contributes to national digital growth.

  • Democratize quality tech education
  • Bridge the industry-academia gap
  • Foster practical, project-based learning

Our Vision

To become Bangladesh's leading catalyst for digital transformation by creating a generation of tech professionals who drive innovation, entrepreneurship, and economic growth.

  • Produce 10,000+ industry-ready professionals by 2026
  • Establish partnerships with 100+ tech companies
  • Expand to 5 major cities across Bangladesh

Our Educational Philosophy

We believe in learning by doing. Every course includes real-world projects that simulate industry challenges, ensuring our students gain practical experience that directly translates to workplace competence.

  • Hands-on coding exercises and assignments
  • Capstone projects for portfolio development
  • Industry-relevant case studies

Our curriculum is continuously updated in collaboration with industry experts to ensure relevance and alignment with current market demands and emerging technologies.

We cultivate a culture of continuous learning through alumni networks, advanced certification pathways, and ongoing professional development opportunities.
