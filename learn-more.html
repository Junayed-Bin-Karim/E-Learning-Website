Transforming Education in Bangladesh

Junayed Academy isn't just another video course website. We are a community-driven ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between university theory and industry reality.

Founded with a vision to make premium tech education accessible, we focus heavily on **Project-Based Learning (PBL)**. Our students don't just memorize syntax; they build real software that solves real problems.
